    Report on the 2nd Workshop on the Perspectives on the Evaluation of Recommender Systems (PERSPECTIVES 2022) at RecSys 2022

    Eva Zangerle, Christine Bauer, Alan Said
    February 2023
    PDF Cite DOI

    Abstract

    Evaluation is a central step when it comes to developing, optimizing, and deploying recommender systems. The PERSPECTIVES 2022 workshop at the 16th ACM Conference on Recommender Systems brought together academia and industry to critically reflect on the evaluation of recommender systems. The primary goal of the workshop was to capture the current state of evaluation from different, and maybe even diverging or contradictory perspectives.
